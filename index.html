About Me


The Spiel

I've been raptly building stuff for the web for about a decade at this point, diverging from an academic background in European Languages to pursue web development as a career around 2 years ago.

Sometimes I make twee jokes about how I speak English, French, Spanish & JavaScript, but generally have the good sense and appropriate level of shame to steer clear.

I live for the Eureka! moments that this line of work feeds to me like an overzealous grandma, but aside from the coding aspect of the job, I also get a real kick out of UX and visual design.

UX planning appeals to my love of judicious problem solving; it's gratifying to identify a requirement and wrap your head around what it means to that piece of work. Some people are doctors and save lives; I'm content to make sure a website isn't some grotesque soup of half-baked ideas.

As an artist, an interest in the visual design is a given - it's cool to see colour theory meet WCAG, and to keep pace with emergent technologies to produce stuff that's not your standard Bootstrap one-pager.

So, that's me; self-taught, broad outlook, bad jokes.
